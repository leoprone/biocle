CONTEXT

Gut health

There is scientific evidence showing how the composition and diversity of the gut microbiota are closely linked to healthy aging and improved metabolic performance, highlighting the importance of targeted interventions to promote a balanced microbiota and gut health as factors for longevity and quality of life. In this context, butyrate plays a key role in intestinal epithelial function and immune regulation, and its supplementation can sometimes be highly beneficial.

However, much of the active compounds such as butyrate are lost before reaching their site of action. Traditional delivery systems for bioactive molecules, like capsules, release the compounds into the extracellular environment of the target area, dispersing them. This non-specific release can cause adverse effects in off-target tissues and also increases the likelihood of premature degradation or elimination of the active compound, often requiring high doses to achieve the desired effect.

CHALLENGE

Precise Delivery

In the field of biotechnology and pharmacology, there is growing interest in more sophisticated delivery systems that allow maximizing the effect of bioactive compounds while minimizing doses.

SOLUTION

We have developed a delivery platform that protects and transports bioactive molecules directly into the interior of the correct cells, increasing efficacy and reducing the necessary dose in formulations.
